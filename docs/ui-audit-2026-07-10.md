# Hyper Trading Agent UI Audit - 2026-07-10

## Design Direction

- Product tone: professional quantitative research workstation, not a marketing landing page.
- Light theme: red primary `#de283b`, cyan accent `#25b1bf`, white/neutral surfaces.
- Dark theme: black/gray surfaces with orange primary `#FF6600`.
- Interaction style: quiet transitions, clear hover/active/focus states, compact data density, no decorative gradients.

## Guardrails For Future UI Work

- Do not add product-facing development notes or implementation explanations.
- Do not introduce hardcoded `sky`, `cyan`, `violet`, `amber`, `emerald`, or legacy orange classes for product surfaces.
- Do not add visible UI text without updating locale files.
- Keep cards at `rounded-lg` or smaller unless a modal or chat bubble has a specific reason.
- Prefer floating menus for model, connector, knowledge base, and tool selectors.
